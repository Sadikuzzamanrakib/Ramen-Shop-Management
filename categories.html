Explore Our Ramen

Egg Ramen


Egg ramen is a warm noodle soup made with savory broth, springy ramen noodles, and topped with soft-boiled or poached eggs for added richness and flavor.

Meat Ramen


Meat ramen is a hearty noodle soup served in flavorful broth, paired with tender slices of meat like pork, beef, or chicken for extra depth and protein.

Fish Ramen


Fish ramen is a light and savory noodle soup featuring a seafood-based broth, complemented with tender fish pieces or fish cake for a fresh ocean flavor.
